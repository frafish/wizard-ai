Wizard AI is the ultimate agentic AI assistant designed to seamlessly integrate into your WordPress and WooCommerce ecosystem.

== Description ==

**Wizard AI** is the ultimate agentic assistant designed to bring true AI automation to your WordPress and WooCommerce ecosystem. Transform your website into an intelligent, self-managing platform that executes code, generates stunning content, and connects seamlessly to external models.

### 💎 The 100% Native WordPress Experience

Unlike generic wrapper plugins, Wizard AI is engineered specifically for the WordPress ecosystem:
* **WordPress 7.0 AI API Ready:** Built directly on top of the futuristic native WordPress Core AI Connectors and Abilities API. It doesn't hack its way into your site; it utilizes standardized, official protocols.
* **Seamless Native UI/UX:** Forget clunky, bloated third-party interfaces. Wizard AI's dashboard, settings, and editor panels are meticulously crafted to blend perfectly into the classic WordPress UI mood. It feels like a feature WordPress shipped out of the box.
* **Strict Standards Compliance:** Written with clean, secure, and modern PHP 8.1 standards. No heavy external frameworks, no performance dragging. Just perfectly compliant, highly optimized code.

### 🚀 The Complete AI Suite

#### 1. 🦸‍♂️ AI Playground (Command Center)
Your extremely powerful, yet perfectly safe, sandboxed command center. Instruct the AI to execute PHP code, run raw DB queries, and manage WP options. Everything is backed up automatically with instant 1-click rollbacks.

#### 2. 🪄 Gutenberg Editor Agent
Meet your new co-writer. A smart, floating chatbot lives right inside your block editor. Type `/prompt` anywhere to visually generate Gutenberg blocks, rewrite entire paragraphs, or autonomously create and attach AI-generated featured images directly to your media library.

#### 3. 🆓 Free-Tier Providers Included
Start automating at absolute zero cost. Wizard AI integrates natively with top AI providers that offer generous free tiers (like Google Gemini and HuggingFace). Experience enterprise-grade AI capabilities without worrying about monthly API bills.

#### 4. 🌍 WPML Auto-Translation
Take your business global effortlessly. With native WPML integration, Wizard AI can automatically detect, translate, and sync your content across multiple languages, keeping your international SEO flawless and up-to-date.

#### 5. 📡 LLMs.txt & Markdown Endpoints (AI-Ready SEO)
Prepare your website for the next generation of search engines. Wizard AI automatically exposes ultra-clean `.md` endpoints for your public content, mapping your entire site architecture via an autogenerated `/llms.txt` file for flawless ingestion by ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity crawlers.

#### 6. 🔗 MCP & OpenAPI Integrations
Take remote control of your WordPress site. Connect Claude Desktop (via the Model Context Protocol) or Custom GPTs (via OpenAPI Custom Actions) to manage your site, read data, and execute tasks securely from your favorite AI clients.

#### 7. 🔔 AI Webhook Receiver
Keep track of everything. Provide external AI agents, Zapier flows, or background cron tasks with a secure REST endpoint that logs their automated actions directly into your native WordPress Comments (cleanly attached to specific post IDs).

#### 8. 🧠 RAG Knowledge Base
Give your AI true, deep context. Automatically sync your posts, WooCommerce products, and user data into a high-performance, local SQLite vector database. Your AI will always know your exact inventory and data.

#### 9. 💬 Frontend Chatbot
Empower your visitors. Deploy a sleek, lightning-fast frontend chat interface powered by your RAG embeddings to provide instant customer support, smart WooCommerce recommendations, and guided user experiences.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/wizard-ai` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Configure your API Keys (Gemini, OpenAI, or HuggingFace) under the AI Connectors menu.
4. Enjoy your new agentic superpowers!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is Wizard AI safe to use? =
Yes. Wizard AI operates with strict boundaries. It cannot modify WordPress core files. All dangerous operations (DB edits, PHP executions) require your approval and offer instant rollback states.
